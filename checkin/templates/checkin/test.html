Open Check In is an open source system designed to streamline attendance for children's ministry and other educational events.

Welcome to {{preferences.church_name}}

Capabilities:

  • Parent profiles
  • Staff check in
  • Intranet-capable
  • Customizeable with your theme
  • Map embed with Open Street Map
  • Works across your entire local network
  • Compatible with open source Kiosks such as Porteus Kiosk
  • Compatible with Android smart phones and tablets as well as Apple iPhone and iPad
  • Compatible with all major operating systems: Linux, Windows, MacOS, and FreeBSD

Open Check In relies on many well-established and trusted open source initiatives: Python, Django, Bootstrap, Popper, Jquery, OpenCV, Pixelcog, Pixabay and more.
